REQUIRED SCHOOL UNIFORM

Junior School

KG - Year 2

  • GIRLS: Red & white checked blouse. Red & white checked skirt with straps attached.

  • BOYS: Red & white checked shirt. Navy blue shorts with no fly but an elasticated top.

  • Black shoes & white ankle socks

  • School badge sewn onto pocket and house colours on epaulettes

Prep School

Year 3 – 6

  • GIRLS: Red & white checked blouse. Red & white checked pleated skirt.

  • Black leather shoes (not trainers) and white ankle socks.

  • BOYS: Red & white checked shirt. Navy blue shorts.

  • Black leather shoes (not trainers) and navy blue/black plain or ribbed short socks.

  • School badge sewn onto pocket and house colours on epaulettes.

Senior School

Year 7 – 11

  • GIRLS: Cream coloured (Tetron cotton Shade No. 1052) short-sleeved school shirt with breast pocket and school badge. Navy blue, knee-length skirt with a rear centre pleat OR navy blue straight-legged and tailored uniform trousers.

  • Black leather shoes (not trainers) and white plain or ribbed short socks.

  • A white or black headscarf is permitted.

  • BOYS: Cream coloured (Tetron cotton Shade No. 1052) short-sleeved school shirt with breast pocket and school badge.

    • Navy blue uniform trousers (YEARS 10 & 11). Navy blue, knee-length uniform shorts (YEARS 7–9).

  • Black leather shoes (not trainers) and plain navy blue/black socks or ribbed short socks.

Year 12 – 13

  • GIRLS: Navy blue, knee-length skirt with a rear centre pleat or navy blue straight-legged and tailored uniform trousers, and blue & white lined blouse.

  • BOYS: Navy blue straight-legged and tailored uniform trousers and blue & white lined shirt.

  • Black leather shoes (not trainers) and white ankle socks.

FOR ALL DEPARTMENTS BOYS & GIRLS

Physical Education and Games

Plain white polo shirt with collars with school badge on pocket. Plain navy blue shorts (just above the knee) P.E. shoes & white socks

  • GIRLS & BOYS: Shin pads and white football socks,Optional: football boots, hockey stick, tennis, badminton and Squash racquets.

Swimming

  • Plain blue/black swimming trunks/costumep>

Cool Weather

  • A plain long or sleeveless navy blue V-neck jumper or cardigan is permitted. Only plain black or navy blue waterproof jackets are allowed in school. School badge sewn onto pocket and house colours on epaulettes.

  • HOUSE T-SHIRTS FOR INTER-HOUSE MATCHES AVAILABLE FROM THE SCHOOL OFFICEli>

Jewellery

One earring stud per ear lobe is permitted for girls only. Wrist watches are permitted and encouraged. One religious symbol is permitted to be worn.

  • NO OTHER JEWELLERY IS PERMITTED
