Admissions

  • Home
  • Admissions

Admission to the school is normally in September. However, for families moving into the area at other times during the year exceptions are usually made and the pupil is admitted into the most appropriate year group, based on age and previous educational achievements.

In the Kindergarten Department, entry is restricted to September only except in the case of overseas pupils who may enter in January.

For Registration for all classes, details of the Registration fee which must accompany the appropriate form, are given in the " Current Information" sheet enclosed with the Prospectus. The Registration fee is not refundable
