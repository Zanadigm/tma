Message From the Head of School

Welcome to The Mombasa Academy!

We, at The Mombasa Academy, ensure high-quality education which motivates and empowers our students to be lifelong learners and productive members of society. We want all our students to achieve their full potential. Our task is to make it possible and our mission is to provide a platform. Success for all students is ensured at our school by delivering an enriching, child-centred, balanced and structured curriculum and a whole school focus on student welfare that focuses on the positive recognition of student achievements. I welcome you all to an outstanding learning community where everyone is dedicated to a high standard of excellence. My commitment to our community is to lead the school with enthusiasm and passion to enable us to reach our goals. We look forward to welcoming you to our vibrant learning community.

Mr. Andrew K Manyasa

Head of School
